Gulaab Jamun

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Bread
  • Oil

Recipe

  1. Mix 1 cup milk powder, 1/4 cup flour, 1/4 tsp baking soda, and 2 tbsp ghee or milk,
    Add warm milk to form a smooth dough.
  2. Boil 1 1/2 cups sugar, 1 1/2 cups water, and crushed cardamom for 5-7 mins. Keep warm.
  3. Roll dough into smooth balls, avoiding cracks.
  4. Fry the balls in hot oil/ghee until golden, then soak them in the warm syrup for 30 mins.

Burfi

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Bread
  • Oil

Recipe

  1. In a pan, boil 2 cups full-fat milk and reduce to a thick consistency.
  2. Stir in 1 cup sugar, 1/4 cup condensed milk, and 1/4 tsp cardamom powder Cook until,
    it thickens further.
  3. Once thick, pour the mixture into a greased tray and spread evenly.
  4. Let it cool completely, then cut into pieces and garnish with chopped nuts.
